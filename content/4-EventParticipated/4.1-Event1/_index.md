### Key Session Highlights

#### Context Is Everything: Making AI Actually Work for You — Tinh Truong

- Why AI fails without context, and what "context" actually means
- From prompts to memory: how AI is evolving (the Second AI Brain concept)
- How better context leads to better results — practical mindset and tips
- Career insights and how students can start building with AI

#### Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q — Anh Pham

- **Quick Chat Agent**: AI assistant for exploring data and analyzing insights
- **Quick Flows**: Create intelligent workflows using natural language — no coding required
- **Quick Spaces**: Shared collaborative spaces that turn individual insights into team knowledge
- **Quick Sight**: Build dashboards and reports from raw data using natural language

#### From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation — Thinh Nguyen

- Amazon CloudFront for every workload type
- Cost optimization with Amazon CloudFront
- Security capabilities of CloudFront
- Enhanced reliability and performance with Amazon CloudFront

#### 36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho — Team VIB

- The journey from zero to idea during a 36-hour hackathon
- Defining the problem and shaping the UTMorpho product
- Building under pressure — challenges, failures, and turning points
- UTMorpho product demo and key learnings

#### Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLM Settings — Duc Dao

- How LLMs select the next token
- The assumption: Temperature=0 guarantees determinism
- The reality: inference optimizations break that assumption
- Practical impacts and mitigation strategies

#### Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System: Startup Credit Scoring — Vy Lam

- The structural mismatch between banking systems and startup data
- Single Agent: when to use it and when not to
- The Multi-Agent paradigm and the Blueprint of a Virtual Credit Committee
- Guardrails, compliance, and operational ROI

### Key Takeaways

**On AI and Context:**
- AI output quality directly depends on the quality of context provided — "garbage in, garbage out" still applies to AI
- Amazon Q's Quick Chat, Flows, Spaces, and Sight features show AI is being deeply integrated into entire enterprise workflows, not just chat tools

**On CloudFront:**
- CloudFront is far more than a CDN — it's a comprehensive foundation for security and performance optimization, directly relevant to the ITCoach project
- How CloudFront helps reduce bandwidth costs and improve response times for end users

**On LLM Non-Determinism:**
- Temperature=0 does not guarantee identical outputs due to hardware-level inference optimizations — critical when designing AI systems that require reproducibility
- Practical mitigation strategies for non-determinism in production systems

**On Multi-Agent Systems:**
- Multi-agent architecture is well-suited for complex problems requiring different types of reasoning
- Guardrails and compliance are non-negotiable when deploying AI in financial domains

### Applying to the ITCoach Project

- Immediately applied the "context is everything" mindset to designing prompts for the Lambda AI Processor — providing full context about the question, candidate level, and evaluation criteria before calling OpenAI
- Improved CloudFront configuration for ITCoach based on what was learned: cache behavior, security headers, cost optimization
- Considering a multi-agent architecture for more complex interview evaluation features in future versions

### Event Experience

This was my first time attending a large-scale AWS community day in Vietnam, with nearly 400 attendees at Bitexco Financial Tower on floor 26. The atmosphere was vibrant and professional throughout.

The most memorable session was Team VIB's share about LotusHacks — hearing a student team recount building a product in 36 hours, from nothing to a live demo, was genuinely inspiring. I am currently in the middle of building ITCoach myself and could relate closely to the technical challenges they described.

The Q&A sessions after each talk gave me the chance to ask speakers specific questions directly. I asked about handling non-determinism in production AI systems and received very practical, hands-on advice.
